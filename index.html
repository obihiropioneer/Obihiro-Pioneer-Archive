Obihiro Pioneer Archive - 「おびひろパイオニア」バックナンバー一覧

This is an archive of back issues of Obihiro Pioneer. For more recent editions, please visit the Obihiro Home Page (link).

Obihiro Pioneer is a newsletter published monthly by the Obihiro City Tourism and Goodwill Exchange Section for residents of the Tokachi District and related parties. Here you can find the current and back issues of Obihiro Pioneer. The contents of this newsletter do not necessarily represent the official views or opinions of the Tourism and Goodwill Exchange Section or the City of Obihiro.
